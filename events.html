Hanami (Flower Viewing)

Sit, relax, and enjoy a meal under the blossoms with family and friends! Limited number of seating available day and night.

Parade

The start of the cherry blossom season kicks off with a grand parade! You won't want to miss this!

Bike Rentals & Tours

Rent a bike and ride around on your own or in a tour to enjoy the blossoms.

Schedule

Performances

Enjoy free live entertainment such as dance, plays and more! Appropriate for all ages.

Schedule

Music

Listen to traditional Japanese music, such as taiko drums, koto, and more!

Schedule

Tea Ceremony

Experience a traditional tea ceremony lead by an experienced instructor! (Reservations in advance are encouraged.)

Schedule

Food Stalls

Sample and savor Japanese food in our large food stall area! Popular options include sushi, tempura, onigiri, takoyaki, yakiniku, sakura mochi, and dango!

Schedule

Arts & Crafts

Browse and purchase handmade Japanese arts & crafts, such as yukata, ikebana, ukiyo-e, and more. Free origami classes for children will be available as well!

Schedule

Omiyage (Souvenirs)

Take home cherry blossom themed snacks and goods for friends, family, or even yourself to enjoy! Vendors selling omiyage can be found throughout the park.
